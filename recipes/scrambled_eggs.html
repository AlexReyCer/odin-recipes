Scrambled Eggs

Easy to make, hard to screw up, and delicious, the scrambled eggs is a recipe that saves you time when you need it the most, and i will show you how to do them.

  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tablespoons sliced Cheddar cheese
  • 2 thin slices ham (Optional)
  • 1 teaspoon heavy whipping cream
  • Â½ teaspoon butter

Steps

  1. Step 1: Whisk eggs together in a small bowl until smooth. Mix in Cheddar cheese, ham, and heavy cream.
  2. Step 2: Melt butter in a skillet over medium heat. Pour in egg mixture; cook and stir until set but still moist, 3 to 5 minutes.
